Spirit If you use a Incense(Smudge Stick) during a hunt it won't hunt for 3min vs. the usual 90 seconds. EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, Ghost Writing
Wraith This ghost will not leave footprints in salt, it can also teleport to you not caring about where you are in the map. EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, D.O.T.S Projector
Phantom Taking a picture during a ghost event makes It invisible in the picture, blinks are also slower which means It disappears longer. Spirit Box, UltraViolet, D.O.T.S Projector
Poltergeist This Ghost loves throwing things, during hunts it will toss things at extreme speeds and distances. Spirit Box, UltraViolet, Ghost Writing
Banshee If you're playing multiplayer the Banshee will targer one specific player during hunts and will not harm any other players. The Banshee also has a chance to scream through the parabolic microphone. UltraViolet, Ghost Orbs, D.O.T.S Projector
Jinn This ghost can not turn off the breaker. When the breaker is on this ghost gains a burst of speed while chasing you. EMF Level 5, UltraViolet, Freezing Temperatures
Mare This ghost hunts in the dark and it is not able to turn on any lights. A giveaway that it's a Mare is that as soon as you turn on a light it will turn it right back off. Keeping the Ghost room lit to see if it hunts outside of the ghost room in the dark it could be a Mare. Spirit Box, Ghost Orbs, Ghost Writing
Revenant This ghost speeds up with line of sight and slows down when the player leaves the ghosts line of sight. Ghost Orbs, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Shade This Ghost will not show itselfor do any interactions when you're inside the Ghost room. When using a summoning circle the ghost will show itself as a shadow before it actual form. EMF Level 5, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Demon This Ghost hunts earlier and more frequently than any other Ghost. It can hunt after 1 minute after being smudged vs the usual 90 seconds, it can also initiate a hunt 20 seconds after its last one. UltraViolet, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Yurei This Ghost will drain your sanity when interacting with doors you are near and also has the ability to fully close the front door without causing a hunt, any other Ghost leaves doors open at least a little bit. Ghost Orbs, Freezing Temperatures, D.O.T.S Projector
Oni Blinks less so It's more visible, no floating ball ghost event. EMF Level 5, Freezing Temperatures, D.O.T.S Projector
Yokai Talking too much or using electrical equipment too much can tripper a hunt earlier than usual, If you're hiding from the ghost and your flashlights start to flicker but the ghost doesn't sense you then it could be a Yokai. Spirit Box, Ghost Orbs, D.O.T.S Projector
Hantu This ghost shows freezing breath during a hunt when the breaker is off, it also gets faster when the temperature drops. UltraViolet, Ghost Orbs, Freezing Temperatures
Goryo Only shows up on DOTS through a monitor so bring a camera and tripod, this ghost also can not switch Ghost rooms. EMF Level 5, UltraViolet, D.O.T.S Projector
Myling You will not be able to hear footsteps until flashlights start to flicker. EMF Level 5, UltraViolet, Ghost Writing
Onryo If the Ghost blows out 3 firelights consecutively and hunts right away it could be a Onryo. Spirit Box, Ghost Orbs, Freezing Temperatures
The Twins This ghost interacts with objects from a distance, during a hunt one twin will exhibit a slower movement speed and the other will be faster. EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, Freezing Temperatures
Raiju Any electrical device near it will make the ghost faster. EMF Level 5, Ghost Orbs, D.O.T.S Projector
Obake The Shapeshifter, this ghost has a chance of shapeshifting during hunts, it also stays invisible longer during the blinks. EMF Level 5, Fingerprints, Ghost Orbs
The Mimic The Mimic shows Ghost Orbsin no evidence runs. This Ghost will also show 3 other pieces of evidence along with the Ghost Orbs. Spirit Box, Fingerprints, Freezing Temperatures
Moroi Gets faster the lower your sanity gets. Incense(Smudge Sticks) blinds them longer (7.5s vs the regular 5s). Spirit Box, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Deogen (My Personal Favorite) You can not hide from this ghost. This ghost is fast when its far away but insanely slow when it's near players. Spirit Box, Ghost Writing, D.O.T.S Projector
Thaye This ghost starts fast and aggressive but weakens overtime, also moves fast without line of sight. Ghost Orbs, Ghost Wrting, D.O.T.S Projector
