Spirit If you use Incense (Smudge Stick) during a hunt, it won't hunt for 3 minutes vs. the usual 90 seconds. EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, Ghost Writing
Wraith This ghost will not leave footprints in salt. It can also teleport to you regardless of your location on the map. EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, D.O.T.S Projector
Phantom Taking a picture during a ghost event makes it invisible in the photo. Blinks are also slower, meaning it disappears longer. Spirit Box, UV Light, D.O.T.S Projector
Poltergeist This ghost loves throwing objects. During hunts, it tosses items at extreme speeds and distances. Spirit Box, UV Light, Ghost Writing
Banshee If you're playing multiplayer, the Banshee will target one specific player during hunts and won't harm others. It also has a chance to scream through the parabolic microphone. UV Light, Ghost Orbs, D.O.T.S Projector
Jinn This ghost cannot turn off the breaker. When the breaker is on, it gains a burst of speed while chasing you. EMF Level 5, UV Light, Freezing Temperatures
Mare This ghost hunts in the dark and cannot turn on lights. A giveaway is when you turn on a light, it immediately turns it off. Keeping the ghost room lit can help identify it. Spirit Box, Ghost Orbs, Ghost Writing
Revenant This ghost speeds up with line of sight and slows down when the player leaves its line of sight. Ghost Orbs, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Shade This ghost will not show itself or interact with objects while you're in its room. When using a summoning circle, it appears as a shadow before its actual form. EMF Level 5, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Demon This ghost hunts earlier and more frequently than others. It can hunt 1 minute after being smudged vs. the usual 90 seconds, and it can initiate a hunt 20 seconds after the last one. UV Light, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Yurei This ghost drains your sanity by interacting with nearby doors. It can also fully close the front door without causing a hunt. Ghost Orbs, Freezing Temperatures, D.O.T.S Projector
Oni Blinks less, making it more visible. Does not perform the floating ball ghost event. EMF Level 5, Freezing Temperatures, D.O.T.S Projector
Yokai Talking or using electrical equipment excessively can trigger an early hunt. If you're hiding and the ghost doesn't sense you despite flickering flashlights, it could be a Yokai. Spirit Box, Ghost Orbs, D.O.T.S Projector
Hantu This ghost shows freezing breath during hunts when the breaker is off. It gets faster as temperatures drop. UV Light, Ghost Orbs, Freezing Temperatures
Goryo Only visible on D.O.T.S through a monitor. It also cannot switch ghost rooms. EMF Level 5, UV Light, D.O.T.S Projector
Myling You won't hear footsteps until flashlights flicker during a hunt. EMF Level 5, UV Light, Ghost Writing
Onryo If the ghost blows out 3 candles in a row and hunts immediately after, it could be an Onryo. Spirit Box, Ghost Orbs, Freezing Temperatures
The Twins This ghost interacts with objects from a distance. During hunts, one twin is slower, while the other is faster. EMF Level 5, Spirit Box, Freezing Temperatures
Raiju Nearby electrical devices increase this ghost's speed. EMF Level 5, Ghost Orbs, D.O.T.S Projector
Obake The shapeshifter. It has a chance to change appearance during hunts and stays invisible longer between blinks. EMF Level 5, Fingerprints, Ghost Orbs
The Mimic The Mimic shows Ghost Orbs in no-evidence runs. It also gives three other pieces of evidence alongside Ghost Orbs. Spirit Box, Fingerprints, Freezing Temperatures
Moroi Gets faster as your sanity decreases. Incense (Smudge Sticks) blinds it for 7.5 seconds instead of the usual 5 seconds. Spirit Box, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Deogen You cannot hide from this ghost. It moves fast when far away but becomes very slow near players. Spirit Box, Ghost Writing, D.O.T.S Projector
Thaye This ghost starts fast and aggressive but weakens over time. It moves quickly without line of sight. Ghost Orbs, Ghost Writing, D.O.T.S Projector
